How does this work?

Twitter allows you to search for tweets via geolocation data such as latitude and longitude. Using these, we can search for commonly tweeted terms at a specific location within a radius.

What happens when I search?

You will be redirected to Twitter with your customized search query. Everyone you see in the timeline will be within your specified location and radius.
